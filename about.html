Our story

EcoTech Solutions was founded in Johannesburg in 2022 by a team of environmental engineers and digital innovators. We saw an opportunity to use technology for measurable sustainability gains while creating long-term value for clients.

Our mission

Provide practical, affordable eco-friendly technology solutions that reduce energy costs, lower emissions, and improve operational resilience.

Our vision

To be Africa’s trusted partner for green infrastructure, smart energy systems, and sustainable digital transformation.

Core values

  • Responsibility: we deliver solutions that are good for people and the planet.
  • Innovation: we combine engineering, renewable systems, and data-driven insights.
  • Partnership: we listen, adapt, and build long-term client relationships.

Who we serve

  • Small businesses improving energy use and reducing operating costs
  • Property developers designing sustainable residential or commercial sites
  • Community organizations seeking low-carbon technology planning
